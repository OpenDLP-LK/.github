## How to Use Templates

### Step 1: Choose the Right Template
Select the template that best matches your discussion type. If unsure, use the general approach or ask in the General category first.

### Step 2: Copy the Template
1. Open the template file
2. Copy the entire content
3. Create a new discussion in the appropriate category
4. Paste the template content

### Step 3: Fill Out the Template
- Replace all `[placeholders]` with your information
- Check the relevant checkboxes with `[x]`
- Remove sections that don't apply (optional)
- Add any additional context

### Step 4: Review Before Posting
Use the checklist at the bottom of each template to ensure:
- ‚úÖ You've searched for similar discussions
- ‚úÖ You've provided sufficient context
- ‚úÖ You've removed sensitive information
- ‚úÖ You've tagged appropriately
- ‚úÖ You've chosen the correct category

### Step 5: Post and Engage
- Post your discussion
- Monitor for responses
- Provide additional information if requested
- Mark helpful answers as solutions
- Update the discussion with your findings

## Template Guidelines

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Be specific**: Provide clear, detailed information
- **Be structured**: Follow the template sections
- **Be respectful**: Follow community guidelines
- **Be responsive**: Engage with responses
- **Be helpful**: Share solutions you find
- **Sanitize data**: Remove sensitive information

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- **Don't skip sections**: Complete all relevant parts
- **Don't share secrets**: No passwords, keys, or sensitive data
- **Don't be vague**: Provide context and details
- **Don't ignore responses**: Engage with helpful answers
- **Don't duplicate**: Search first before posting
- **Don't post real personal data**: Use sanitized examples

## Customizing Templates

Feel free to adapt these templates to your specific needs:
