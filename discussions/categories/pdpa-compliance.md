**Description:** Discussions about Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) compliance, regulations, and best practices specific to Sri Lanka.

## Purpose

This category is dedicated to helping Sri Lankan organizations understand and implement PDPA requirements in their DLP programs. Share experiences, ask questions, and collaborate on compliance strategies.

## Topics Covered

### Legal and Regulatory
- PDPA Act No. 9 of 2022 interpretation
- Data protection authority guidelines
- Legal obligations for data controllers and processors
- Cross-border data transfer requirements
- Consent management and documentation
- Rights of data subjects (access, rectification, erasure)

### Compliance Implementation
- PDPA compliance checklists
- Data protection impact assessments (DPIA)
- Privacy by design principles
- Record of processing activities (ROPA)
- Breach notification procedures
- Compliance monitoring and auditing

### Organizational Requirements
- Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointment and responsibilities
- Employee training and awareness programs
- Privacy policies and notices
- Data processing agreements
- Vendor and third-party compliance
- Documentation and evidence management

### Industry-Specific Guidance
- Financial services compliance
- Healthcare data protection
- E-commerce and online services
- Telecommunications sector
- Government and public sector
- Education institutions
